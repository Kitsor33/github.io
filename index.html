CITY

Warsaw

The most dynamic city in Poland, which is famous for its museums, palaces and rich in cultural life. Historic buildings are harmoniously combined with modern architecture, and skyscrapers rise above the tiled roofs of the restored Old Town.

Milan

Milan enjoys fame as one of the world's centers of the fashion and design industry. Milan is also home to one of the world's most famous opera houses, La Scala. More than 2 million tourists visit Milan every year.

Munich

Here is the concern of the famous automaker. The city has a BMW Museum, which can be called a paradise for boys from 5 to 50 years old. The museum is built in the shape of a gas tank cap. Here you can see the very first models of BMW cars, and the latest innovations.

Rome

The famous Forum, the legendary Colosseum, the ancient Pantheon and many other grandiose ruins. Rome is also cozy street cafes and trattorias, famous squares and fountains, palaces and gardens.

Paris

The capital of France is famous for its majestic architecture, legendary fashion houses, perfumes and unique French cuisine. And the history of Paris began in the III century BC, with a small settlement on the Île de la Cité on the Seine River.
